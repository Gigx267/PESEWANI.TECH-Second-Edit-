Policy Scope

This Acceptable Use Policy ("Policy") governs the use of all products and services provided by Pesewani Tech ("Company", "we", "us"), including but not limited to: SaaS platforms, POS systems, APIs and integrations, cloud-hosted services, cybersecurity solutions, websites and mobile applications.

This Policy applies to all users, clients, partners, employees, and third parties (collectively, "Users").

3. Prohibited Activities

Users must not engage in any of the following:

a. Illegal or Unlawful Activities

  • Violating Ghanaian law or international regulations
  • Facilitating fraud, money laundering, or financial crime

b. Security Violations

  • Unauthorized access or attempted access to systems
  • Bypassing authentication or security controls
  • Introducing malware, ransomware, spyware, or malicious code
  • Conducting penetration testing without written authorization

c. Data Misuse

  • Unauthorized collection, storage, or sharing of personal data
  • Processing personal data without legal basis or consent
  • Breaching confidentiality obligations

d. System Abuse

  • Excessive or abusive use that degrades system performance
  • Automated scraping without permission
  • Interfering with service availability or uptime

e. Intellectual Property Violations

  • Infringing copyrights, trademarks, or proprietary rights
