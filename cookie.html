Managing Cookies

Browser Settings

Users may control or delete cookies through their browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block or allow all cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites
  • Auto-delete cookies when closing browser

Important Note

Disabling cookies may affect platform functionality. Some features may not work properly if cookies are blocked, particularly:

  • Authentication and login systems
  • Shopping carts and e-commerce functions
  • Personalized settings and preferences
