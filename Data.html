Roles & Responsibilities

C

Controller Responsibilities

The Controller shall:

  • Ensure a lawful basis for processing
  • Provide required notices and obtain consent where applicable
  • Ensure data accuracy and lawfulness
P

Processor Responsibilities

Pesewani Tech shall:

  • Process personal data only on documented instructions
  • Ensure confidentiality of authorized personnel
  • Implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures
  • Assist the Controller with data subject requests
