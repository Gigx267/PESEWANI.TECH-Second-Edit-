FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process.

What's included in a site assessment?

Our comprehensive assessment includes infrastructure audit, security vulnerability analysis, workflow evaluation, and a detailed report with recommendations and implementation plan.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary: Office setups (1-2 weeks), Security installations (3-7 days), Policy development (2-4 weeks). We provide exact timelines after initial assessment.

Do you offer maintenance contracts?

Yes, we offer flexible maintenance packages including 24/7 monitoring, regular updates, and priority support. Contact us for custom packages.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Accra, Kumasi, and Takoradi, but accept projects nationwide. Remote consultations are available for clients outside our primary service areas.

Can you work with existing infrastructure?

Absolutely. We assess and optimize existing systems, integrate new solutions, and ensure everything works seamlessly together.

What's your response time for emergencies?

Emergency support clients receive immediate response (under 30 minutes) and on-site support within 2 hours for critical issues in our service areas.
